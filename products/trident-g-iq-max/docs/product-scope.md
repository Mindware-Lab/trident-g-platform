# Product Scope

`trident-g-iq-max` is the expanded full-game product in the new sibling product line.

It includes:

- Hub
- Tests
- Zone Coach
- Capacity Gym
- Reasoning Gym
- Missions
- Progress
- Coach Review

It builds on the same shared shell as `trident-g-iq-basic`, but extends that shell into the broader reasoning and mission layers described in the legacy prototype and ground-truth materials.
