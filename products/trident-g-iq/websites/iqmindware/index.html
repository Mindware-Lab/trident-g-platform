Founder and scientific lead

Dr Mark Ashton Smith

Dr Mark Ashton Smith is a cognitive neuroscientist trained at the Center for the Neural Basis of Cognition (University of Pittsburgh and Carnegie Mellon University), and former lecturer and researcher in the Department of Psychology at the University of Cambridge.

His work on transferable cognitive interventions was synthesised into the Trident G computational framework, which informs IQMindware protocol design and proof posture.
