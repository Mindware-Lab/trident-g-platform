How far transfer works

Built to carry over under changed conditions

Horizontal transfer

The same underlying cognitive demand is trained across changing surface forms — letters, locations, objects, and relations — so skill is not locked to one wrapper.

Vertical transfer

Reasoning demand rises progressively from recognition through relational binding to fluid inference, building capacity that generalises upward.

Delayed re-checks

Gains are tested under changed conditions and after time — not only immediately after training — so you can verify whether improvements hold in real tasks.

The programme

One programme, four training layers

Trident G IQ Pro combines assessment, readiness regulation, capacity training, and reasoning training inside one coach-led transfer framework.

Explore the programme →
Tracker
Pre/post SgS-12 fluid reasoning snapshots and Psi-CBS progress checks.
Zone Pulse
3-minute readiness check that gates and routes each daily training session.
Capacity Gym
Adaptive N-back and relational N-back training with horizontal transfer protocol.
Reasoning Gym
Relation matching, constraint solving, and must-follow inference with vertical transfer protocol.
Modes: Coach-led mode for the structured 20-day programme. Manual mode for self-directed practice and review.
