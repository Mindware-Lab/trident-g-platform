Getting started

For first sessions and basic fit checks.

Yes. Current IQMindware apps are configured for both desktop/laptop and mobile/smartphone use.

Start on Start here. The baseline path is: G Tracker baseline, Zone Coach readiness check, then one Capacity Gym block if state is viable.

A first pass is usually 15 to 30 minutes. Ongoing usage can be shorter and scheduled around your actual work week.

How training can help

A quick practical summary of expected benefits when the workflow is used consistently.

The system is designed to build general cognitive efficiency in attention control, working memory, and reasoning quality, then verify carryover under changed conditions.

  • Stay effective under distraction and interruption.
  • Use AI without eroding independent reasoning.
  • Prevent throughput burnout from constant acceleration.
  • Build cognitive reliability across demanding days.
  • Make better decisions under uncertainty and time pressure.
