Practical guidance

What this means for your decisions

1
Start with baseline context

Run a clean readiness check before you start demanding training or deep work.

2
Follow a steady cadence

Follow the output route: proceed, go lighter, reset first, or run a quick re-check.

3
Interpret trends, not one-offs

Track repeated outputs over time so decisions become more personalized and stable.

4
Keep claims in scope

Use Zone Coach for day-to-day regulation, not as a diagnostic label for health conditions.
