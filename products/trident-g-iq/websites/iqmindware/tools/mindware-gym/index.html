What Reasoning Gym does

The vertical transfer layer

Capacity Gym prepares the run-time control system. Reasoning Gym then asks you to use that control for rule-based thinking, slot resolution, and logical inference — the higher-order reasoning target.

Task families

What you practise

  • Relation matching — identify the correct relational structure between items
  • Constraint solving — resolve a set of rules to reach a valid conclusion
  • Must-follow reasoning — determine what must be true given the premises
Form switching

Real-world and nonsense forms

The coach alternates between tasks with familiar real-world meanings and tasks with nonsense or abstract content. This ensures the reasoning skill is not dependent on prior knowledge or surface familiarity — it has to work from structure alone.

Vertical progression

From recognition to inference

The coach moves from simpler recognition tasks toward deeper relational binding, multi-step rule application, and fluid reasoning challenges as the programme progresses. The difficulty increases as your capacity base builds in Capacity Gym.

Coach-led routing

Combined with Capacity Gym

In Coach-led mode, Reasoning Gym sessions are combined with Capacity Gym sessions in the same daily block. The coach decides the balance and sequence based on your Zone Pulse output and programme position. In Manual mode, you can access Reasoning Gym tasks independently.

How it fits

Reasoning Gym in the 20-day programme
