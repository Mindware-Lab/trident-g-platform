# IQMindware homepage copy deck

This file rewrites the homepage around the current offer:

**one integrated, coach-led 20-day programme** inside the Trident G IQ Pro app, rather than the older multi-app launch-pass framing.

The aim is to make the homepage clearer on:
- what the product is
- how it works
- why it is different
- how to start
- how proof is handled
- how coaching fits around the programme

---

## Strategic direction

Shift the homepage from:

**"Here are our separate apps and prices"**

To:

**"Here is the Trident G IQ Pro programme, how it works, why it is different, and how to start."**

This means:
- keep the **transfer** message
- keep the **proof** message
- keep the **readiness / state** message
- keep the **coach-led** structure prominent
- demote the old standalone-app framing
- remove the old launch-pass pricing block
- replace it with a cleaner **programme + coaching routes** section

---

# 5. Assess, regulate, train, then re-check

## Eyebrow

HOW IT WORKS

## Heading

**Assess, regulate, train, then re-check**

## Step 1

### Label

**Assess**

### Copy

Run a short baseline check in Tracker before the programme begins and again at the end to compare pre/post performance.

### Time

about 5 min

## Step 2

### Label

**Regulate**

### Copy

Use Zone Pulse before training to estimate readiness and decide whether today should be a full route, a reduced route, or a lighter stabilising session.

### Time

about 3 min

## Step 3

### Label

**Train**

### Copy

Complete today’s coach-led route through Capacity Gym and Reasoning Gym, with workload adjusted for level, state, and progression.

### Time

10 to 20 min

## Step 4

### Label

**Re-check**

### Copy

Use delayed checks and repeat Tracker measures to test whether gains hold under changed conditions and across time.

### Time

about 5 min
