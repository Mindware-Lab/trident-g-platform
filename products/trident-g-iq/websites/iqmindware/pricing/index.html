Live coaching

Add Live Coaching

Coaching is optional and runs alongside the app. All sessions are led by Dr Mark Ashton Smith — cognitive neuroscientist, formerly at the University of Cambridge — who reads your Zone Pulse trends and Tracker data directly to guide your route through the programme.

What coaching adds

The app's Coach-led in-app mode routes you automatically. Live coaching adds a human expert who reviews your actual data, adjusts your approach, identifies friction points you may not notice yourself, and helps you transfer gains into real-world contexts.

Three coaching phases

Sessions follow a structured arc: map your specific friction pattern, train capacity and build scripts, then test transfer under real conditions. Every session is recorded and sent to your inbox.
