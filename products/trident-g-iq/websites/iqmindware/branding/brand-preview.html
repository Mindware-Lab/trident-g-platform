Three states
§ State cards — coloured top border · sdot · state-tag
State 01
Flat / Dull
Low cognitive arousal. Working memory bandwidth is compressed. High-load training now will produce noise, not signal.
→ Recovery block
State 02
In the Zone
Optimal readiness. Attention control is high, interference is manageable. This is the window for hard relational work.
→ Challenge block
State 03
Spun Out
Over-aroused or cognitively loaded. Performance will look fast but errors are high and consolidation is unlikely.
→ Stabilise first
§ Steps — 4-col · right-border dividers How it works

Four steps
to real readiness

01 — Check
Measure your state
Run a 3-minute CCC readiness check. The app outputs your brain state clearly: Flat, Zone, or Spun Out.
02 — Route
Get routed correctly
The coaching layer selects the right session type for your state. No guessing. No defaulting to the same routine.
03 — Train
Work in the right zone
Adaptive n-back with controlled perturbation. The system tests whether your gains survive representation changes.
04 — Track
See real progress
Block-level history, variance trends, and CCC output over time. Evidence you can see and export.
