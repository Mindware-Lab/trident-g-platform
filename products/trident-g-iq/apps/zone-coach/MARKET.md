Zone Coach Standalone (Pricing + Positioning)

## What is Zone Coach?
Zone Coach is a short daily cognitive readiness check you run before training or deep work.

The current app uses a fast behavioural probe based on a masked majority-direction task
(MFT-M / CCC-style probe) to estimate Cognitive Control Capacity (CCC) and route the user
to a practical next-step recommendation.

It is similar in spirit to athletes using HR/HRV for training decisions, but designed for
cognitive performance:

- A fast behavioural CCC probe (masked majority-direction task)
- A simple cognitive state label (for example: In the Zone / Flat / Locked In / Spun Out)
- A confidence estimate and validity checks (to avoid over-interpreting poor runs)
- A clear recommended next cognitive block (work / training / intervention guidance)
- Local trend tracking (CCC bits/second over time)
- Future optional sensor extensions (HRV first, potentially EEG later)

Zone Coach is a training-readiness and self-regulation tool. It is not a medical device.

## Who is it for?
- Knowledge workers who want a reliable "am I trainable right now?" check
- Students and high performers who want to reduce "bad state training"
- Creators/executives who want more consistent deep work sessions
- People focused on cognitive longevity who want a simple daily readiness and trend tool
- People who may later want to combine behavioural readiness with HRV (and eventually EEG) trends

---

## What makes it different (vs generic HRV apps)
Most HRV apps give a readiness score based on physiology only.

Zone Coach adds:
- Behavioural throughput and control signals (CCC bits/second + performance signatures)
- A state-routing layer that translates performance patterns into actionable guidance
- State-matched next-step recommendations (work / training / intervention)
- Local trend signals linked directly to performance, not only physiology
- A future pathway to mind-body coupling (behaviour + HRV, and later EEG)

---

## What users get (current core loop)
1) Optional pre-check self-label (how you feel right now)
2) CCC check (masked majority-direction probe; about 3 minutes)
3) Validity/status result (for example, if the run was incomplete or disrupted)
4) Brain State Result:
   - CCC bits/second
   - Cognitive State
   - Confidence Level
5) Mechanism summary + Recommended Next Cognitive Block (1-4 hours)
6) Click-through guidance cards for:
   - Cognitive Training
   - Work
   - Other Intervention
7) Local 24-day CCC graph ("See Data"), with an estimated In the Zone band once enough data exists

Also supported:
- Quick re-check (75s) after an intervention or reset

## Pricing Bands (positioning draft)
A simple model with no subscription.

### Zone Coach Basic
Includes:
- CCC behavioural probe (full check + quick re-check)
- State label + confidence level + validity checks
- Mechanism and next-block recommendation
- Guidance cards (training / work / intervention)
- Local-first history and CCC trend graph

### Zone Coach Pro (future)
Everything in Basic, plus:
- Sensor pairing (BLE) for compatible HR devices
- HRV-derived trend features (for example, DFA alpha1-style readiness trends)
- Behaviour + HRV trend dashboard
- Correlation views over time (for example, HRV trend features vs CCC bits/second / stability)

### Optional: EEG Expansion Pack (future / exploratory)
Adds:
- EEG device integration (where supported by platform/SDK)
- EEG trend features (exploratory performance-oriented metrics)
- Combined mind-body trend views (behaviour + HRV + EEG)
- Correlation views over time (EEG <-> HRV <-> CCC performance signals)

Note: EEG features would be exploratory biometrics for performance training, not clinical metrics.

## What you can safely claim (and what you should not)
### Safe positioning
- "Daily cognitive readiness check"
- "Cognitive state routing before deep work or training"
- "More consistent training and work sessions"
- "Local trend tracking of cognitive performance over time"
- "Optional future mind-body trend tracking"

### Avoid
- "Diagnoses"
- "Treats insomnia/burnout/pain"
- "Medical-grade brain criticality"
- "Guaranteed IQ increases"

## Why it should sell
People already pay for:
- Wearables and straps
- HRV apps
- Focus and productivity tools
- Brain-training subscriptions

Zone Coach is the missing layer that:
- checks readiness with a behavioural cognitive probe
- translates results into a clear next step
- protects training quality (not just quantity)
- builds a personal trend profile over time

It is "readiness gating for cognition", with practical recommendations.
