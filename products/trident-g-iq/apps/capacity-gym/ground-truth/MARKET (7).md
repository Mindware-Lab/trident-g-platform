Capacity Gym (Positioning + Market)

## What is Capacity Gym?
Capacity Gym is a local-first cognitive training app focused on working memory, attention control, and relational processing.

It combines:
- adaptive n-back training
- relational reasoning n-back variants
- a coach layer designed to reduce "thin automation" (game-specific habits that do not generalize)

Capacity Gym is a performance training tool. It is not a medical device.

---

## What games does it offer?
Capacity Gym currently includes:

1. Hub n-back (3-modality):
- categorical wrapper (`hub_cat`)
- non-categorical wrapper (`hub_noncat`)
- cue-driven target modality (location / colour / symbol)
- adaptive difficulty with controlled interference

2. Relational n-back modes:
- Transitive (order + equality bridge)
- Graph (directed edge relations)
- Propositional (inference-rule premises: MP / MT / DS)

These relational modes are distinctive because matching is based on canonical meaning, while the surface can vary.

---

## What makes it different?
Most brain training apps optimize for near transfer:
- users get better at the exact task format they practice.

Capacity Gym is designed to increase the likelihood of far transfer by training invariance:
- same deep relation, changing surface form
- wrapper changes and controlled perturbations
- coach-driven recovery vs challenge routing
- quiz probes that test structure understanding, not just reaction habits

In short, it targets robust control policies, not only faster game-specific patterning.

---

## The Trident G Far Transfer Protocol (coaching layer)
The coaching logic implements a practical "stability -> perturbation -> selection -> consolidate" cycle:

1. Stabilize signal quality:
- detect messy blocks and route to recovery blocks
- protect learning quality before increasing challenge

2. Controlled perturbation:
- adjust one dial at a time when possible (for example speed, interference, wrapper)
- use swap probes to test whether performance survives representation changes

3. Selection pressure against thin automation:
- if performance collapses under a swap or pulse, route back to stabilize and retry
- if performance holds, keep or promote the stronger setting

4. Consolidation:
- after strong blocks, avoid random disruption and consolidate gains

This protocol is designed to improve the odds that progress is portable beyond the exact training wrapper.

## Why relational n-backs matter
Relational modes are not just "harder n-back." They train:
- structure tracking across changing surface forms
- retrieval of deep relations under time pressure
- consistent attention and working memory while representations shift

That aligns with the type of relational processing often associated with fluid reasoning in real tasks.

## User value proposition
Capacity Gym gives users a practical system to:
- improve working memory and attention efficiency
- stay in a productive challenge zone without constant overload
- train for portability, not only in-game score gains
- build relational processing fluency that should transfer better to everyday reasoning demands

The claim is not guaranteed IQ increase. The claim is a science-based protocol intended to increase the probability of meaningful carryover.
