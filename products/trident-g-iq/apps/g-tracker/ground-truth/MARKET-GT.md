G Tracker Battery (Positioning + Market)

## What is G Tracker?
G Tracker is a local-first assessment battery for tracking meaningful cognitive outcomes across a training cycle.

It combines:
- Pre/post outcome tests (to show change)
- Repeatable weekly trackers (to monitor trajectory)
- Optional external benchmark checks

The product goal is simple: move from one-off scores to usable evidence of change in intelligence, applied intelligence, resilience, and AI-enabled performance.

G Tracker is a performance tracking tool. It is not a medical device.

---

## The unique proposition
Most tools measure one slice only:
- IQ snapshot only, or
- mood/wellness only, or
- productivity proxy only.

G Tracker combines five high-value layers in one workflow:

1) **Reasoning capacity (fluid g)**
- Short g Scale (SgS-12 A/B) pre/post forms

2) **Applied general intelligence in real-world settings**
- Psi-CBS Core (repeatable)

3) **Cognitive health and resilience under pressure**
- CRS-10 (repeatable) plus Psi-CBS-AD option

4) **Decision quality habits (transfer to everyday choices)**
- EDHS A/B pre/post forms

5) **AI-use intelligence**
- Psi-CBS-AI option to track whether AI usage is improving or degrading cognitive performance quality

This gives users a practical “capacity + application + resilience + execution + AI leverage” profile, not just a single static number.

## What users get (current battery structure)
### Pre- and post-training outcome tests
- **Valid 12-item IQ (SgS)**: Form A at baseline, Form B at post
- **Everyday Decision Habits (EDHS)**: Form A at baseline, Form B at post
- **Optional external Mensa benchmark**: Form A then Form B later

### Repeatable trackers
- **Psi-CBS** (weekly/fortnightly/ad-hoc): applied intelligence, with optional AD and AI-use modules
- **CRS-10** (weekly/fortnightly/ad-hoc): cognitive resilience under pressure

### In-app tracking flow
- Clear status states (Not started / In progress / Due now / Up to date)
- Baseline vs post comparison panels
- Repeatable trend sparklines and due cadence
- Local export/import and reset controls

## Why this should sell
People already pay for:
- IQ tests
- Productivity tools
- Journaling/behaviour tracking apps
- Brain-training programmes

G Tracker solves the missing piece: **credible, repeatable evidence of whether training is working in the outcomes users care about.**

It is designed to answer:
- Did reasoning capacity improve?
- Did daily cognitive bandwidth improve?
- Did resilience under pressure improve?
- Did decision habits improve?
- Is AI use improving performance quality or weakening it?

## Positioning vs alternatives
### Versus one-off IQ tests
- Adds repeated applied-performance and resilience tracking
- Adds pre/post structure and longitudinal interpretation

### Versus generic wellness trackers
- Anchors changes to cognitive outcomes and test structure
- Includes objective-style reasoning snapshots plus behavioural self-report trackers

### Versus standalone training apps
- Provides a shared evidence layer across multiple outcomes, not just in-task scores

- “Medical treatment”
- “Guaranteed IQ increase”
- “Validated for all high-stakes selection decisions”
