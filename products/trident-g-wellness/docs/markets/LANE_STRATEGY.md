# Lane Strategy

## Lane Catalogue

1. `longevity_global`
2. `longevity_sa`
3. `corporate_sa`

## Activation Sequence

Phase 1:

1. Activate `longevity_sa` first for fast validation.
2. Run 60-90 day evidence sprint:
   - routing adherence
   - recovery speed trend
   - cognitive stability trend
   - retention and weekly completion
3. Produce case summaries and evidence pack.

Phase 2:

1. Activate `corporate_sa` with local procurement flow.
2. Reuse proven protocol engine, adapt onboarding, copy, and dashboard packaging.

## Why This Order

- Lower sales friction and faster cycle time in consumer lane.
- Fewer procurement/privacy blockers while model is still being tuned.
- Stronger corporate positioning once outcome evidence exists.
