# Brand and IP Boundary

## Separation Rules

1. Trident G Wellness and Trident G IQ operate with separate branding and public positioning.
2. Wellness-facing assets must not imply they are part of the Trident G IQ mission pages or app suite.
3. Customer terms/privacy pages must be lane/product specific.

## Shared Protocol License

1. Protocol logic (CCC + ANS routing framework) can be licensed into the wellness product.
2. License grant must define:
   - scope of use
   - territory/lanes
   - modification rights
   - attribution requirements
   - revocation terms

## Claims and Safety

1. Wellness product remains non-medical.
2. High-impact claims require evidence gating and manual approval.
3. Publish blocks apply when claims are unsupported, over-generalized, or high-risk.

## Data and Compliance

1. Keep workspace-level separation in backend records.
2. Keep customer support and finance logs partitioned by workspace/product.
3. For SA corporate procurement, provide local payment options where possible.
