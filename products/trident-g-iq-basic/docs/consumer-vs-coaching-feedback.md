# Consumer vs Coaching Feedback

Consumer-facing Capacity feedback should stay lean during active play.

## Active HUD

Show only:

- Block Type
- N-back
- Accuracy
- Transfer Score
- Credit
- Blocks Left

`Credit` means session-earned **g plasticity cells**. Do not show coaching-only submetrics during active trials.

## Right Strip / Sheet

Show richer feedback outside the active trial window:

- Session g plasticity cells.
- Total g plasticity cells.
- Derived IQ credits at `100 g = 1 IQ credit`.
- Transfer Score and label.
- Stable level.
- Rolling accuracy and rolling Transfer Score.
- Family progress.
- Last block result.

## Coaching-Only Detail

Keep detailed lapse patterns, swap-cost analysis, and internal portability diagnostics out of the active HUD. They can appear in coaching exports or internal review views if needed.
