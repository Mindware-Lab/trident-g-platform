Brain states

Three states. One right move for each.

State 01
Flat
Low arousal, disengaged executive system. Your prefrontal capacity is dampened — not the time for high-load tasks. Rest, recharge, and prime for re-entry.
→ Restore protocol
State 02
In the Zone
Optimal arousal, fluid executive control. Your working memory and attentional systems are at full coherence. This is your window — go deep, perform.
→ Full training green
State 03
Spun Out
Over-activated, dysregulated arousal. Cortisol-driven noise swamps signal. Cognitive control collapses under pressure. De-escalate first.
→ De-load protocol

The process

From check to action in under five minutes

01 / LOG
Note your felt state
Before the probe, mark how you feel — Flat, In the Zone, or Spun Out. Metacognitive calibration starts here.
02 / PROBE
Run the 3-min CCC check
A validated task battery assessing executive control, inhibition, and working memory. Do not switch tabs during the run.
03 / SCORE
Get your CCC score
Instant output — your Cognitive Control Capacity score mapped to one of three state categories.
04 / ACT
Follow the recommendation
A clear next step: full training, moderate session, or recovery protocol. Matched to your score and history.
