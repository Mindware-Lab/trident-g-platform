# RESEARCH_AND_EVIDENCE_POLICY.md

## Evidence posture

- Prefer demonstrated measurement behavior over promise language
- Keep validation status explicit per module
- Mark in-testing measures clearly

## Independent validation

Independent validation and conceptual replication studies are encouraged.

## Minimum publication standards

- Methods and caveats are inspectable
- Aggregate reporting only
- No user-level publication
- Reproducible schema contracts for published aggregates

## Corrections

If published summaries require correction, issue dated update notes in `changelog/RELEASE_NOTES.md`.
