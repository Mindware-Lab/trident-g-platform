# CLAIMS_AND_SAFETY.md

## Allowed claims

- Designed to support training quality and transfer-relevant outcomes
- Carryover is tested under change where protocol specifies
- Validation status is explicit and module-specific

## Not allowed

- Clinical diagnosis/treatment claims
- Guaranteed outcome claims
- Blanket "validated for all high-stakes decisions" claims

## Safety statement

This project is for training, research, and educational use.
It is not medical advice and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease.
