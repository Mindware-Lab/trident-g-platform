# AI_USE_AND_HUMAN_OVERSIGHT.md

## Human-led AI policy

AI may assist drafting, analysis, and summarization, but accountability remains human-led.

## Minimum controls

- Human review before public release
- No autonomous publication of evidence claims
- No generation of user-identifiable outputs
- Explicit versioning of AI-assisted policy/docs changes

## Boundaries

- AI outputs must not introduce diagnostic or guaranteed-outcome claims
- AI use must preserve module-level caveats and validation status transparency
