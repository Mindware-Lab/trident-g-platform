# CONTRIBUTING.md

Thanks for helping improve transparency, validity, safety, and reproducibility.

## What we welcome

- Corrections to protocol clarity and definitions
- Improvements to schema quality and auditability
- Privacy and safety hardening suggestions
- Reproducibility and evidence-governance improvements

## Contribution boundaries

Please do not submit:
- Runtime app code
- User-level data
- Identifiable clinical or personal data
- Medical or diagnostic claims

## PR expectations

- Keep PRs small and focused
- Describe expected vs observed behavior
- Include rationale and references for evidence-related edits
- Ensure docs remain consistent with non-clinical claims boundaries

## Evidence and claims policy

Do not add language implying guaranteed outcomes or clinical effects.

Use conservative framing aligned with:
- training, research, and educational use
- module-specific validation status
- aggregate-only evidence publication
