## 3) Free Substack About page

### Recommended version
**IQ Mindware is a public lab for transferable intelligence.**

**I write about how to train general intelligence capacity and portable thinking skills in a way that takes transfer seriously: not by assuming gains carry over, but by asking how they should be tested under changed conditions and what should count as credible evidence.**

**This publication sits at the intersection of cognitive science, AI and cognition, the future of work, entrepreneurship, and practical mental performance. A central question runs through it: how do we build reasoning, strategic action, resilience, and creativity in ways that remain usable beyond one task, one wrapper, or one good day?**

**That question matters even more now. Work is becoming faster, more AI-mediated, and more cognitively uneven. Analytical thinking, judgement, and portable strategy are still valuable, perhaps more than ever, but they are also easier to offload, distort, or erode under overload. I write about that changing landscape too: what AI is doing to knowledge work, how cognitive offloading affects critical thinking, and why resilience and smart work now matter as much as raw effort.**

**Alongside those essays and commentaries, I also explain the IQ Mindware framework:**
- **Zone** for state regulation and workable cognitive range  
- **Capacity** for training core control and transferable capacity under load  
- **Mindware** for portable strategy, decision, and problem-solving scripts  
- **G-Loop** for turning solving, checking, and feedback into reusable capability  

**You can expect a mix of framework explainers, research synthesis, higher-level essays, commentary on the future of work, short takes on AI and cognition, and a few teaser applications drawn from the broader IQ Mindware and Trident-G approach.**

**The aim is simple: to explore how transferable intelligence might be trained, tested, and applied with more clarity, more honesty, and more practical value than most of this space usually offers.**
